Professional Experience

Autonomous Off-road Navigation

Oct. 2023 – Present: I excited to contribute to the TartanDrive project at AirLab (Robotics Institute of Carnegie Mellon University), where we are developing an autonomous all-terrain vehicle that can navigate in challenging environments. My role includes integrating thermal imaging into vehicle's systems and developing learning-based visual and perception models to enhance navigation precision.

Cardiovascular Flow Analysis with MTV

Jun. 2021 – Aug. 2023: For three summers, I assisted Dr. Kartik Bulusu in cardiovascular research at Biofluid Dynamics Laboratory. We applied MTV techniques to analyze fluid flow in human artery models. My role included leading the design and preparation of experiments. In collaboration with Dr. Charles Fort, we gathered initial data on 'MTV to Measure Wall Shear Stress in Model Cardiovascular Flows' in October 2022. I also presented our findings at the APS 2022 Fluid Dynamics conference in Indianapolis, IN.


Delivery Drone Charging System

Jan. 2022 – Sept. 2022: During my internship at Drone Point Solutions, LLC, I designed a charging system for delivery drones. This involved researching EV charging innovations and learning about battery management systems and technology.
