Professional Experience

Cardiovascular flow analysis with MTV

Jun. 2021 – Present : Over the last two summers, I have assisted Dr. Kartik Bulusu with cardiovascular research and implementing the MTV experimental technique to extract high-precision fluid flow data from a human artery model. I made contributions through a review of recent relevant literature and the preparation and execution of experiments. In October 2022, in collaboration with Charles Fort and his group, we collected initial experimental data on the subject of MTV to Measure Wall Shear Stress in Model Cardiovascular Flows. I had an opportunity to attend APS 2022 Fluid Dynamics in Indianapolis, IN, where we presented our progress.

Delivery drone charging system

Jan. 2022 – Present : In 2022, I had an opportunity to intern at a start-up, Drone Point Solutions LLC, to design a drone charging system specifically designed for package delivery drones. I researched innovations and standard practices regarding battery charging and energy management. Relevant topics also included the configuration of EV charging systems and cars. As a result, I learned about battery management techniques, battery technology, and charging systems.
